About Our Church

Founded in 1982 and constituted in 1985, Hmong First Baptist Church is affiliated with the Southern Baptist Convention through the Minnesota-Wisconsin Baptist Convention, the Hmong Baptist National Association, and the Lakeland Baptist Association. Our desire is to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ among Hmong families in Milwaukee and beyond.

Our Mission

To proclaim the gospel, teach God’s Word, and serve our community in the love of Christ.

Our Worship

We gather on Sundays for prayer, Bible teaching, and congregational singing in Hmong and English.
